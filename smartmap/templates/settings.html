Geolocation

Depending on the purpose of your website, you may need to use geolocation to automatically determine where your site visitors are located. Smart Map provides the following options:

  • None

    Geolocation is disabled.

  • FreeGeoIp.net

    A free service, FreeGeoIp.net can perform IP address lookups automatically.

  • MaxMind

    A paid subscription service, MaxMind provides more accurate results, and a greater guarantee of uptime.

WARNING

FreeGeoIp.net may experience unexpected downtime, which could have a negative impact on your website! It is highly recommended not to rely on FreeGeoIp.net.

If you require geolocation, MaxMind is a far more reliable service.
